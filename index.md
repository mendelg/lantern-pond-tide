# Lantern Pond

**A zen ripple puzzle. You never touch the lantern — you touch the water.**

Lantern Pond is a calm, one-finger puzzle game for iPhone and Android. Hold a finger to gather a wave, let it go, and guide a paper lantern across a moonlit pond to its shrine.
