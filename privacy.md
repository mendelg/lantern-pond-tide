# Lantern Pond — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Lantern Pond collects no personal data. None.

- No accounts. The game never asks who you are.
- No analytics, no advertising, no tracking SDKs of any kind.
- Your progress (stars, fireflies, settings) is stored only on your device.
- Purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play;
  we never receive your payment details.
- At launch the app downloads one small file of level definitions from
  GitHub ("The Tide Pool"). This is an anonymous download, like loading a
  web page; no identifier or personal information is sent with it.
- Since we collect nothing, there is nothing to retain, sell, share,
  or delete.
